01

about

I am a cybersecurity student in my final year of a BSc at Hoyskolen Kristiania, Bergen campus. I work at the intersection of offensive security and software development: I like understanding exactly how a system fails, and then writing the code that makes the next failure visible.

I compete in CTFs, mostly crypto, pwn, and forensics, and I volunteer as a developer at Kvarteret studenthus. My current focus is whether AI coding tools actually produce secure code, the subject of my bachelor thesis and the idea behind Riposte, an offline purple-team platform I build solo.
